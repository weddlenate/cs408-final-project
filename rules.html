How to Play Tetris

  1. Move the pieces left and right with the 'A' and 'D' keys
  2. Place the piece at the bottom of the board with the 'S' key
  3. Rotate the pieces clockwise or counterclockwise with the left and right arrow keys
  4. Stack the blocks efficiently, leaving as little space as possible
  5. Make complete horizontal lines on the board to remove rows and get points
  6. Don't let the blocks stack to the top of the screen
  7. Go for as long as possible!
